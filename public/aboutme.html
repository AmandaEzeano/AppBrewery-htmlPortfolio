About Me

I firmly believe that web development is a form of storytelling. Each website I create is an opportunity to engage users in a narrative that unfolds with every interaction. Collaboration is at the heart of my approach, as seen in my role as a HelpDesk Analyst at WaterlooIT, where I proactively deliver IT solutions and ensure client satisfaction.
